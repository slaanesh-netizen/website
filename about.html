About M&R Books

A more personal way to keep your books organized.

M&R Books is a boutique bookkeeping and accounting support business for small businesses, entrepreneurs, and individuals who want clear records, responsive communication, and fewer financial loose ends.

We intentionally keep our client list focused so each client receives attentive support, reliable routines, and work that is easy to understand. The goal is not to make bookkeeping feel bigger. It is to make it feel manageable.

Why we stay small

Better support starts with having room to pay attention.

Bookkeeping touches cash flow, deadlines, payroll, taxes, and the decisions people make every day. M&R Books is not built to process as many files as possible. We work with a focused roster so we can understand the details, notice what needs attention, and communicate before small issues become stressful ones.

Founder story

Built for clients who want to feel looked after, not processed.

Megan started M&R Books after seeing how quickly bookkeeping can become stressful when people feel behind, unsupported, or unsure what their numbers are telling them. Her approach combines full-cycle bookkeeping experience with a calm, organized process that helps clients understand what is happening and what needs to happen next.

M&R Books is not a CPA firm. We provide bookkeeping and accounting support, and we can help keep your records organized so your CPA or tax preparer has cleaner information when it matters.
